    New committer: Senmiao Liu

    2024 Jun 25

    The Project Management Committee (PMC) for Apache Kyuubi has invited Senmiao Liu to become a committer and we are pleased to announce that he has accepted.

    Senmiao Liu has been contributing to Apache Kyuubi for 3 years, including feature implementation, docs/testing improvement, bug fixing, etc. In particular, he has contributed to multiple engines such as Spark, Trino,and JDBC.

    Being a committer enables easier contribution to the project since there is no need to go via the patch submission process. This should enable better productivity. A PMC member helps manage and guide the direction of the project.

    Please join me in congratulating Senmiao Liu!
