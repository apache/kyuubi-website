    New committer: He Zhao

    2025 Jan 2

    The Project Management Committee (PMC) for Apache Kyuubi has invited He Zhao to become a committer and we are pleased to announce that he has accepted.

    He Zhao works at Cisco, and he has been contributing to Kyuubi for one and half years, he made major contributions in the Kyuubi Web UI and Kyuubi Grafana dashboard.

    Being a committer enables easier contribution to the project since there is no need to go via the patch submission process. This should enable better productivity. A PMC member helps manage and guide the direction of the project.

    Please join me in congratulating He Zhao!
